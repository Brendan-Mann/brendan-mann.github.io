Climatology: METR 327

Hurricane Precipitation
Analysis

Western Illinois University  ·  Spring 2026

Two maps applying the same pipeline — MRMS Multi-Sensor QPE raster data, IBTrACS storm track records, and Lambert Azimuthal Equal Area projection — to two of the most destructive Atlantic hurricanes in modern U.S. history. Harvey (2017) and Helene (2024) represent contrasting storm behaviors: Harvey stalled over Houston producing historic rainfall concentration, while Helene tracked inland delivering catastrophic precipitation across the Southern Appalachians.
