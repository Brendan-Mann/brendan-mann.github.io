GIS 309: Thematic Mapping

Mass Shootings
in America

Western Illinois University  ·  February 2026

Four maps, four datasets, four definitions — the same phenomenon mapped differently. This project compares how The Violence Project, Mother Jones, the Mass Shooting Tracker, and the AP/USA Today mass killings database each produce a distinct spatial picture of gun violence in the United States, demonstrating how data provenance and definitional choices fundamentally shape cartographic output.
