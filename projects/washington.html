GIS 309 — Thematic Mapping

Washington State
Mapping Series

Western Illinois University  ·  Spring 2026

A comprehensive seven-map series using Washington State as the study area, spanning environmental analysis, physical geography, and socioeconomic patterns. Each map demonstrates a distinct thematic technique — choropleth, bivariate, proportional symbol, dot density, and demographic centroid analysis — applied to real datasets from federal and state sources.
